# SponsorBlock Category Explanations

A reader-friendly summary of the SponsorBlock submission categories used by iSponsorBlockTV. Use this as a quick reference when deciding which categories to enable, what each one skips, and where the boundaries are.

> **Source:** Adapted from the official SponsorBlock guidelines at <https://wiki.sponsor.ajay.app/w/Guidelines>. The original wiki is the authoritative reference — consult it for edge cases and policy updates.

---

## Quick Reference

| Category | Skips | Typical example |
|---|---|---|
| Chapter | Section labels (not skipped) | "Chapter 2: Setup" |
| Endcards/Credits (Outro) | End-of-video credits and endcards | YouTube endscreen with "more videos" |
| Exclusive Access | Full-video label for sponsored access | Brand-funded review video |
| Highlight | Jumps to the main point of the video | Auto-jump to the reveal |
| Hook/Greetings | Narrated trailer, hello/goodbye | "Hey everyone, today we're…" |
| Interaction Reminder | Like / subscribe / follow nudges | "Smash that like button" |
| Intermission/Intro Animation | Recurring intro animation, still frame, or clip | Channel logo splash |
| Music: Non-Music Section | Non-music gaps in music videos | Talking before song starts |
| Preview/Recap | Up-front teaser or "previously on…" recap | "Coming up on this episode…" |
| Sponsor | Paid promotion of an unrelated product/service | "This video is brought to you by NordVPN…" |
| Tangents/Jokes | Non-essential side content, B-roll, gags | Fake sponsor bit, slow-mo replay |
| Unpaid/Self Promotion | Creator's own merch, Patreon, charity drives | "Check out my new T-shirt store…" |

## Main Categories

### Chapter
Section labels for navigation. Descriptive only — not skipped.

### Endcards / Credits (Outro)
Credits and endcards near the end of the video.

- May absorb **Interaction Reminders** and **Self Promotion** when a clean cut between them isn't possible.

### Exclusive Access (Full Video Label only)
Whole video is built around a product, service, or location the creator received free or subsidized access to. Cannot be removed because it's the entire video — so it's a label, not a skip.

### Highlight
Jumps the viewer straight to the main point of the video.

- **Limit: one per video.** Pick by priority: the video's main point → reference in the title → reference in the thumbnail → reference in the preview.

### Hook / Greetings
Narrated trailer for the video, plus greetings and goodbyes.

- ❌ Don't mark: closing remarks that contain real information / conclusions.

### Interaction Reminder (Subscribe)
Explicit reminders to like, subscribe, or follow the creator on any platform (paid or free).

- ❌ Don't mark: category-specific plugs (e.g. "subscribe to my Patreon") — those belong in **Unpaid/Self Promotion**.
- May be combined into **Endcards/Credits** at video end.

### Intermission / Intro Animation
Recurring segments at the start (or between sections) of a video — animation, still frame, or clip that appears in other videos too.

- ✅ Includes: livestream pause screens, recurring copyright disclaimers.
- ❌ Excludes: skits, time-lapses, slow-motion clips, one-off viewer-protection disclaimers, anything containing speech.

### Music: Non-Music Section
Only used on music-focused videos. Marks gaps that are **not** part of the official or Spotify release of the song.

- Segment must contain only material absent from the official release (typically silence or talking).

### Preview / Recap
A collection of clips showing what's coming up, or a "previously on" recap where the information is repeated elsewhere in the video.

- ❌ Don't mark: recaps that appear only once, or clips drawn from unrelated videos.

### Sponsor
Part of a video promoting a product or service **not directly related to the creator**, where the creator is paid in money or product.

- ✅ Mark: paid third-party ad reads, embedded ad spots.
- ❌ Don't mark: entire videos that are themselves the ad — use the **Full Video Label** instead.

### Tangents / Jokes
Tangential scenes not required to understand the main content.

- ✅ Includes: B-roll, fake sponsors, slow-motion replays, jokes.
- Information-relevance rule does **not** apply here — tangents are skippable by definition.

### Unpaid / Self Promotion
Two related cases bundled together:

- **Self Promotion** — promotion of something **directly related to the creator** (their merch, Patreon, second channel, monetized platform).
- **Unpaid Promotion** — shout-outs, charity drives, or plugs the creator gets no payment for.

May be combined into **Endcards/Credits** when they appear at the end and can't be cleanly separated.

## Segment Types

### Full Video Labels
Applied to the whole video instead of a segment. Available for:

- **Exclusive Access**
- **Sponsor**
- **Unpaid/Self Promotion**

### Mute Segment
Use when the **visuals** contain information worth keeping but the **audio** belongs in another skip category. Also useful to smooth over rough visual transitions.

---

## Submission Guidelines (when reviewing / voting)

Downvote or avoid segments that:

- Don't fit their declared category.
- Include main-content information.
- Cover the entire video when the topic is niche (use a Full Video Label instead).
- Have poorly-timed or non-seamless transitions.
- Stack on top of existing bad segments instead of replacing them.
- Skip non-fullscreen visual overlays when there's no audio reason to skip.
